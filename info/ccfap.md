# Accepted Service Needs 

* Option A: Every Caretaker must have at least one of these:
    * A job
    * self employed 
    * actively looking for work
    * attending school or traiing
    * participating in Reach UP
    * has special needs
* Option B: child has:
    * health or developmental needs 
    * open case with DCF and child care is part of safety plan 
* OPTION C: family has:
    * family is experiencing significant stress in shelter, safety, emotional stability, etc.
